UFO History   >   Events   >   Alien Reproduction Vehicle (ARV) Displayed at Norton Air Force Base

Mark McCandlish reports that his colleague Brad Sorenson visited an Air Force show at Norton Air Force Base in November 1988. In a separated area, he witnessed three so-called alien reproduction vehicles, which resembled UFOs in appearance - they appeared to be well-worn and not brand new. McCandlish theorised that the technology was based on charged electrical plates.
